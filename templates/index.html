About Us

Our Mission: "Matching vibes and syllabuses to help you crush finals together."

AcademiConnect is built for students who are serious about their studies but refuse to compromise on connection. We match you based on course overlap and learning preferences while protecting your privacy with our built-in Ghost Mode.
