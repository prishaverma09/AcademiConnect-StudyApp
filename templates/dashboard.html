🌟 About AcademiConnect

AcademiConnect is an all-in-one academic ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between students. Whether you need a peer to study for finals, a mentor to guide your career path, or a shared library to access course materials, we've got you covered. This platform combines Real-time Presence, AI-ready tools, and Collaborative Whiteboards to make learning social and efficient.

🛠️ How to Use
1. Connect

Use "Find Buddies" to match with students taking your exact courses.

2. Collaborate

Open the Whiteboard or start a chat to work on problems in real-time.

3. Grow

Ask questions in the Library or find a Mentor to master complex topics.
